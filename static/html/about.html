About Elephant vs Donkey

This project is a political resource website with user-driven content. “A political website for people that hate politics.” The purpose is to have a user-friendly website where users can see diverse political information that consists of upvoted comments. The site will span from local to national politics, covering both individual politicians and any local or national voting issue. There will be an emphasis on citing sources (much like Wikipedia) so that the comments will be as validated and accurate as possible.
